UPE IO

UPE IO is a unique opportunity for students to participate in industry related talks, targeted at technologies that aren't typically accessible to students at RPI. These talks benefit them not only as programmers, but as students, and employees as well.

The next scheduled talk is on Wednesday, February 20th, at 6:00pm in Lally 102, and will be on the topic of Android development.

If you want to get a head start before the talk, please download & install the Android SDK. An Android phone emulator is bundled with the SDK, but if you own an Android device and a Micro-USB cable, please bring them with you.
